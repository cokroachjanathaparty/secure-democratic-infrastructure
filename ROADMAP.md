
# Roadmap

# Secure Democratic Infrastructure (SDI)

This roadmap outlines the strategic development direction for Secure Democratic Infrastructure (SDI).

The roadmap is intended to guide:

- engineering priorities,
- research initiatives,
- infrastructure development,
- governance evolution,
- and public transparency efforts.

---

# Guiding Objectives

SDI aims to build:

- transparent democratic infrastructure,
- cryptographically verifiable elections,
- privacy-preserving audit systems,
- distributed constitutional trust,
- and resilient national-scale voting infrastructure.
